Declaration of Absence of Risks
Waiver of the Risk Management Program - PGR
Regulatory Standard No. 01 (NR-01)
DIR No. 001/2026

Eu, [RESPONSIBLE], [POSITION] of the company [LEGAL NAME], registered under CNPJ No. [CNPJ], classified as [TYPE], headquartered at [ADDRESS], primary CNAE [CNAE], DECLARE for all purposes, under penalty of law, that:

  1. The company identified above does not have employees with a CLT employment relationship registered in the Work and Social Security Card (CTPS), or has only the owner-partner in a non-CLT work regime, not falling under the mandatory PGR due to the number of employees.
  2. The company does not develop activities or operations with flammable, explosive, hazardous products or industrial processes subject to specific high-risk Regulatory Standards that impose mandatory PGR preparation.
  3. After analyzing the activities carried out, it is found that there is an absence of occupational risk factors that require the implementation of the Risk Management Program (PGR), according to criteria established in NR-01 and other technical standards of the Ministry of Labor and Social Security.
  4. The company commits to formally notify to the competent body of the Labor Inspection System (SIT) or accredited entity, within 30 (thirty) days, any change in the conditions declared herein that implies the obligation to prepare, implement, and maintain the PGR.

As an expression of truth, I sign this DECLARATION OF NON-EXISTENCE OF RISKS (DIR), which exempts the company from preparing the PGR, based on art. 163 of the Consolidation of Labor Laws (CLT) and Regulatory Standard No. 01 (NR-01), approved by MTb Ordinance No. 1.186/2018 and subsequent amendments.
