Digital Inclusion

Accessibility Policy

We recognize the importance of digital accessibility. We are committed to providing an inclusive experience for all users, without exception.

1. Compliance Level

This site is designed to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA . We also proactively apply several Level AAA best practices to provide maximum inclusion. Our efforts include high color contrast, native screen reader support, and perceptible visual focus control.

2. Implemented Accessibility Features

  • Accessibility Bar: Dynamic controls for font size, contrast inversion, and text spacing.
  • Inclusive Typography: Option to activate the OpenDyslexic font, specially designed for users with dyslexia.
  • Skip Links: Hidden "Skip to main content" links at the top of the page, facilitating keyboard navigation.
  • Auditory Feedback (ARIA): Intensive use of attributes aria-live , aria-hidden e role to ensure that assistive technologies fully understand calculators and dynamic menus.
  • Structural Semantics: Clean hierarchical HTML organization (H1 to H6 tags, main, nav, article), essential for correct systemic reading.

3. Keyboard Shortcuts and Systemic Navigation

The site is structured to be 100% operable without a mouse. For quick navigation, we provide the following commands:

  • Tab: Moves focus sequentially between interactive elements (links, buttons, calculator inputs).
  • Shift + Tab: Returns focus to the previous element.
  • Enter / Space: Activates the button, performs the calculation, or opens the focused link.
  • Esc (Escape): Closes open menus, modal windows, or selection boxes instantly.
  • Arrow Keys: Used to navigate within form fields, tabs, or dropdown lists.

4. Screen Reader Compatibility

We regularly validate our interface with the most widely used assistive technologies on the market:

  • NVDA (Windows)
  • JAWS (Windows)
  • TalkBack (Android devices)
  • VoiceOver (iOS and macOS devices)
