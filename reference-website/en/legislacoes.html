Nursing Legislation and Technical Opinions

What are Legislation and Technical Opinions?

Nursing legislation is the set of laws, decrees, resolutions, and norms that regulate the nursing profession. Technical opinions are official documents prepared by competent entities that guide the professional practice of nurses. They clarify doubts about procedures, competencies, ethics, and legislation applicable to nursing. They are essential to ensure patient safety and the legality of acts performed by nursing professionals.

ANVISA - National Health Surveillance Agency

Regulatory body responsible for health surveillance in Brazil

ANVISA Opinion on Cold Chain
2020 ANVISA Immunization
This technical opinion establishes guidelines and orientations for the proper functioning of the cold chain, responsible for the storage and conservation of immunobiologicals throughout the supply chain. The cold chain is essential to ensure the efficacy and safety of vaccines, maintaining controlled temperature from manufacturing to patient administration.
Access Opinion
Opinion on Child Vaccination Schedule
2023 ANVISA Pediatric Immunization
This document presents updated recommendations for the childhood vaccination schedule, including new vaccine additions to the National Immunization Program (PNI). The opinion guides health professionals on vaccination schedules, intervals between doses, contraindications, and special situations in the pediatric population.
Access Opinion
Opinion on Adolescent Vaccination Schedule
2023 ANVISA Adolescent Immunization
Specific guidelines for the adolescent vaccination schedule, including immunizations such as HPV, meningococcal, hepatitis B, and necessary boosters. The document also addresses the importance of vaccination adherence in this age group and strategies to overcome access barriers.
Access Opinion
Opinion on Adult Vaccination Schedule
2023 ANVISA Adult Immunization
This technical opinion guides the recommended vaccination schedule for adults, including booster doses and vaccines specific to this age group. It addresses the importance of vaccination against influenza, pneumococcal, hepatitis B, and other vaccine-preventable diseases that predominantly affect adults and the elderly.
Access Opinion
Opinion on Elderly Vaccination Schedule
2023 ANVISA Elderly Immunization
Document establishing specific vaccination recommendations for the elderly, a population more vulnerable to complications from infectious diseases. Includes guidance on vaccines such as influenza, pneumococcal, herpes zoster, and booster of other immunizations, considering immunosenescence and prevalent comorbidities in this age group.
Access Opinion
Opinion on Pregnant Women Vaccination Schedule
2023 ANVISA Gestational Immunization
This technical opinion presents guidelines for vaccination during pregnancy, protecting both mother and baby. Includes recommendations on vaccines such as influenza, hepatitis B, diphtheria, tetanus, and pertussis (dTpa), in addition to guidance on contraindicated vaccines during this period.
Access Opinion

COREN - Regional Nursing Council

Body for inspection and regulation of the profession in the states

COREN Opinion on Use of Gloves for Injection Administration
2021 COREN-SP Patient Safety
This technical opinion clarifies when glove use is mandatory during injectable medication administration. The document provides guidance on situations involving risk of exposure to body fluids, protection of the professional and patient, and correct hand hygiene practices when glove use is not indicated.
Access Opinion
COREN Opinion on Emergency Cart Check
2020 COREN-SP Emergency
This document establishes parameters for systematic emergency cart checking, including frequency, nurse responsibilities, checklist of mandatory items, medications, and equipment. Proper checking is essential to ensure readiness for care in cardiopulmonary arrest situations.
Access Opinion
COREN Opinion on Endotracheal Suctioning
2019 COREN-SP Intensive Care
This technical opinion provides guidance on the nurse's competence in performing endotracheal suctioning, including techniques, frequency, indications, contraindications, and complications. The document also addresses nursing prescription for this procedure and team training.
Access Opinion
COREN Opinion on Parenteral Nutrition
2018 COREN-SP Nutritional Therapy
This document clarifies the nurse's competencies in caring for patients on parenteral nutrition, including central venous access management, solution preparation and administration, complication monitoring, and role in the multidisciplinary nutritional therapy team.
Access Opinion
COREN Opinion on Nasoenteral Tube Insertion
2019 COREN-SP Nutritional Therapy
This technical opinion provides guidance on insertion, fixation, and position verification of nasoenteral tubes. It includes indications, insertion technique, position confirmation by clinical and complementary methods, and guidance on enteral diet administration.
Access Opinion
COREN Opinion on Indwelling Urinary Catheter Insertion
2020 COREN-SP Urology
This document establishes guidelines for indwelling urinary catheterization, including indications and contraindications, male and female insertion technique, catheter size and type selection, proper fixation, and prevention of catheter-associated urinary tract infections.
Access Opinion

COFEN - Federal Nursing Council

Highest regulatory body of the profession in Brazil

Law No. 7.498/1986 - Nursing Creation Law
1986 COFEN Professional Regulation
This law regulates the practice of nursing professionals, including nurses, nursing technicians, nursing assistants, and midwives, establishing the competencies, duties, and responsibilities of each professional category. It is the fundamental legal framework defining the scope of nursing practice in Brazil.
Access Law
COFEN Resolution No. 564/2017 - Code of Ethics for Nursing Professionals
2017 COFEN Professional Ethics
The Code of Ethics for Nursing Professionals establishes ethical duties and prohibitions that regulate professional conduct. This document is essential to ensure responsibility, dignity, and excellence in nursing care delivery, protecting both the professional and the patient.
Access Code of Ethics
COFEN Resolution No. 543/2017 - Parameters for Nursing Staff Sizing
2017 COFEN Staffing
This resolution establishes parameters for nursing staff sizing in health services, defining productivity indices, proportion between professional categories, and calculation methodology. Adequate sizing is essential to ensure patient safety and quality of care.
Access Resolution
COFEN Resolution - Competencies of Each Nursing Professional
2022 COFEN Professional Competencies
This document clearly defines the specific competencies of each professional nursing category (nurse, technician, and assistant), establishing exclusive and shared activities. The definition of competencies is fundamental for organizing teamwork and ensuring quality of care.
Access Resolution
COFEN Guide for Medication Preparation, Administration, and Monitoring
2017 COFEN Medication Safety
This guide establishes guidelines for the safe preparation and administration of medications by the nursing team, including the nine rights, dose calculation, dilution, routes of administration, incompatibilities, and monitoring of adverse reactions. It is essential for preventing medication errors.
Access Guide
COFEN: Safe Practices for Medication Administration (2018)
2018 COFEN Patient Safety
This document deepens safe practices for medication administration, addressing patient identification technologies, barcode medication administration, medication reconciliation, and strategies for error reduction. The focus is on implementing safety barriers in the medication chain.
Access Document
COFEN: Medication Administration at the Appropriate Time (2017)
2017 COFEN Medication Safety
This document addresses the importance of administering medications at the correct prescribed time and the therapeutic implications of delays or early administration. It includes guidance on prioritizing critical medications and strategies to ensure timeliness in administration.
Access Document
Safe Use of Medications - Guide for Preparation, Administration, and Monitoring (Fiocruz)
2021 Fiocruz/COFEN Medication Safety
This guide, developed in partnership between Fiocruz and COFEN, presents evidence-based practices for the safe use of medications, covering from preparation to therapeutic monitoring. It includes care flowcharts, checklists, and guidance for special situations.
Access Guide

Ministry of Health

Federal agency responsible for national health policy

Law No. 8,080/1990 - Organic Law of the Unified Health System (SUS)
1990 Ministry of Health Health System
This law, known as the Organic Law of SUS, provides for the conditions for the promotion, protection, and recovery of health, and the organization and functioning of corresponding services. It is the fundamental legal framework of the Unified Health System, establishing its principles, guidelines, and competencies.
Access Law
Law Creating the Family Health Unit (ESF)
1994 Ministry of Health Primary Care
This legislation establishes the Family Health Strategy as a primary care model in Brazil, setting the minimum team composed of a physician, nurse, nursing assistant, and community health agents. The ESF is the main device for reorganizing primary care and proximity to the population.
Access Law
Law Creating Community Health Agents
2006 Ministry of Health Human Resources
This law regulates the profession of Community Health Agent, establishing their duties, requirements for exercising the function, and labor rights. The CHA is fundamental for the link between the community and health services, conducting home visits and health promotion actions.
Access Law
Law Creating SINAN and Compulsory Notifications
1990 Ministry of Health Epidemiological Surveillance
The National System of Notifiable Diseases (SINAN) is the instrument for collecting and processing data on compulsorily notifiable conditions. This document establishes which diseases and events must be mandatorily reported by health services, essential for monitoring and controlling epidemics.
Access Law

SBIM - Brazilian Society of Immunizations

Scientific society specialized in immunizations and vaccines

SBIM Vaccination Schedules - Brazilian Society of Immunizations
2024 SBIM Immunization
The SBIM vaccination calendars are scientific references that guide vaccination for all age groups, from birth to old age. They are prepared by immunization specialists and updated according to new scientific evidence and the incorporation of new vaccines into the Brazilian market.
Access Calendars
SBIM Official Positions on Vaccination
2024 SBIM Immunization
SBIM periodically publishes official positions on controversial or emerging vaccination topics, such as recommendations for risk groups, new vaccination schedules, and guidance for special situations. These documents are references for healthcare professionals throughout Brazil.
Access Positions

IBSP - Brazilian Institute for Patient Safety

Organization specialized in patient safety

Patient Safety Protocols - IBSP
2020 IBSP Patient Safety
IBSP develops and disseminates patient safety protocols based on international evidence and adapted to the Brazilian reality. These protocols cover areas such as patient identification, hand hygiene, safe surgery, fall prevention, pressure ulcers, and other adverse events.
Access Protocols
Hand Hygiene Protocol - IBSP
2020 IBSP Infection Control
This protocol establishes the indications and techniques for hand hygiene for healthcare professionals, including the alcohol-based hand rub technique and handwashing with soap and water. Proper hand hygiene is the most effective measure for preventing healthcare-associated infections.
Access Protocol

FIOCRUZ - Oswaldo Cruz Foundation

Scientific institution reference in public health and research

Portal of Good Practices in Women's, Children's, and Adolescent Health (IFF/Fiocruz)
2021 Fiocruz Clinical Practices
The Portal of Good Practices of the National Institute of Women's, Children's, and Adolescent Health (IFF/Fiocruz) gathers evidence-based guides, protocols, and educational materials for nursing assistants, nurses, and obstetricians. It covers from prenatal care to newborn care.
Access Portal
Nursing Guides - Fiocruz
2021 Fiocruz Clinical Practices
Fiocruz provides several technical nursing guides developed to standardize procedures and ensure care quality. These materials are important references for continuing education and professional updating of Brazilian nurses.
Access Guides
